Hospital is a premier 700 bedded hospital in the heart of Ghaziabad. To provide quality in general, specialist and super-specialty medical care at highly subsidised rates to one and all is the motto. Its is an endeavour at Santosh hospital to ensure that every person has access to health care facilities when in need and in doing so, to help further establish a healthcare system that attends to the needs of our developing country. OUaRR FACILITIES: Santosh Hospital has spacious wards separate for all specialties, 10 major state-of-the-art Operation Suites and minor Operation suites for each surgical department. It also has well equipped Premature Nursery Ward for babies, besides special care wards NICU (Neonatal Intensive Care Unit) & PICU (Paediatric Intensive Care Unit). Adding to the list, the hospital also has 20 bedded State of the Art ICU for the critically ill patients. Highly Skilled team of doctors and paramedics availability make it one of the best Intensive Care Units around. The ICUs are equipped with advanced life saving, therapeutic and monitoring equipment’s. Not only is our medical staff well-trained and equipped at handling toughest of situations, our paramedical staff too is sought after with a lot of training...."
